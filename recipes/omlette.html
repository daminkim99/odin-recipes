Omlette

Let's make omlette!

Elena's best omlette recipe!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Peppers
  • Tomatoes
  • Mushroom
  • Bacon
  • tsp of salt
  • Oil
  1. First, cut up the veggies you would like to put in your omlette, such as tomatos, peppers, mushrooms,etc.
  2. Now add in some eggs, like three in a bowl, mix it with grain of salt.
  3. With a frying pan, heat it up and put some oil, then pour all the eggs n veggies into the pan.
  4. Change the heating to medium, and wait until veggies and eggs are cooked.
