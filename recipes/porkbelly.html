Pork Belly

Let's fry Pork Belly!

Elena's best pork belly recipe!

Ingredients

  • pork belly
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Season the pork belly with salt and pepper.
  2. Adjust the heat to high and put the pork belly
  3. Once one side seems kind of brown, adjust the heat to medium and flip
  4. Cook the other side, and cut them accordingly.
