Smoothie

Let's make Smoothie!

Elena's best smoothie recipe!

Ingredients

  • 160ml milk
  • 140g frozen berries of your choice
  • Well Riped Banana
  • Protein powder (if want)

Steps

  1. Throw 140g of frozen berries of your choice into a mixer.
  2. Add 160ml of milk into the mixer
  3. Add whole banana, cutting into thirds
  4. Mix well and enjoy!
